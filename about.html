My Story

Elton Jili is a born and bred Durbanite. In his free time Elton will likely be found outdoors hiking up a mountain or going on
a camping Trip. Elton has been in the photography industry for over 2 years and has a variety of experience in different
fields. I am seemingly ‘unflappable’ in stressful situation and always manages to capture great moments. His calm, fun nature
lends itself perfectly to putting all clients at ease. Elton specialises in capturing ‘real life’ moments as they happen and fades into
the background of functions as much as possible.
